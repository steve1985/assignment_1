Kilimanjaro

Africa's highest peak

  • Mount Kilimanjaro is a dormant volcano located in Kilimanjaro Region of Tanzania. Its height is 5895 m.

  • It is the highest mountain in Africa and the highest single free-standing mountain above sea level in the world.

  • The mountain is part of Kilimanjaro National Park and is a major hiking and climbing destination.

  • Because of its shrinking glaciers and ice fields, which are projected to disappear between 2025 and 2035, it has been the subject of many scientific studies.

  • Kilimanjaro has two distinct rainy seasons, one from March to May and another around November.
