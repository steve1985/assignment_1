Chimborazo

The farthest point from the Earth's center

  • Chimborazo's height is 6,263 m, it is the the highest mountain in Ecuador.

  • The mountain is an inactive stratovolcano situated in the Cordillera Occidental range of the Andes.

  • Its summit holds the distinction of being the farthest point on Earth's surface from the Earth's center, due to its location along the planet's equatorial bulge.

  • Owing to this position, the summit of Chimborazo experiences slightly reduced gravity.
